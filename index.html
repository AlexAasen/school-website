Hi, my name is Alexandra Aasen, I'm a creative developer with a love for programming and art.

I live together with my boyfriend and our cat Zoey in Stockholm, Sweden. My spare time is spent painting and trying to be the very best at Heroes of the Storm while Zoey tries to eat my mouse cord. I also work part-time at Gamestop during the weekends.

The main reason I got interested in the art of programming is that I love to bring ideas to life. I also enjoy solving the various problems that occur during the development process.

Two years from now I hope to be a professional web and android developer. It is my dream to be able to split my time between designing and developing for the web and for android.

I'm specialised in

Interaction design

I have a background in game design, so creating an enjoyable experience for the user is always the main focus for me. I believe that a good design is one that feels intuitive, one where the user is guided through his/her experience without being told what to do.

Prototyping

With design, illustration and system-development as parts of my tool-kit I am able to prototype and test ideas in different ways. It also helps me create the best possible solutions based on each field's requirements for a specific project.

Illustration

Illustration is my childhood passion. During my spare time I create and sell my artworks at different events throughout the year. Even though it's mainly a hobby my illustrative, and my image-editing skills have proven fundamental to my work as a designer and a developer.
