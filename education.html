Uppsala University

Game design and Art, 2011-2014

My time at Uppsala university was spent creating games. Each project consisted of 2-3 graphics students pairing up with 2-3 programmers to create something fun. The time spent with each project taught me the importance of each member in a team, and how important it is for me as an individual to be effective in my work and to never miss a deadline. It also made me more flexible as a developer as you often found yourself in situations where you had to rethink your entire game. Either because the game wasn't fun to play or because the team ended up not having enough experience to implement certain features.

I wrote my thesis on inclusive design, how to design a strong, interesting female character and how to avoid hypersexualization.
